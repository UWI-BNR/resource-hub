    Reporting Improvements¶

    Check this page for weekly updates on refit progress

    This section summarises the reporting improvements taking place under Stage 1 of the BNR Refit.
    They focus on transforming outputs from manual reports to clear, consistent, and automatable publications.
    These early steps connect the redesigned analytics with transparent reporting templates that will evolve into a fully integrated reporting system in future stages.
